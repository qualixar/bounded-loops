# Data Processing Agreement

This Data Processing Agreement ("DPA") supplements the underlying services
agreement between Controller and Processor and governs the processing of
personal data carried out by Processor on behalf of Controller.

## Subject Matter of Processing

This DPA covers the processing of personal data necessary for Processor to
provide the hosting and analytics services described in the underlying
services agreement.

## Duration of Processing

Processing shall continue for the term of the underlying services
agreement and shall cease upon termination, subject to the data return and
deletion provisions herein.

## Nature and Purpose of Processing

The nature of the processing includes storage, retrieval, and analysis of
personal data for the purpose of providing the contracted services to
Controller.

## Type of Personal Data and Categories of Data Subjects

The personal data processed includes contact details and usage data
relating to Controller's employees and end users.

## Obligations of the Controller

Controller shall ensure it has a valid legal basis for the processing and
shall provide instructions to Processor consistent with applicable data
protection law.

## Confidentiality

Processor shall ensure that persons authorized to process personal data
have committed themselves to confidentiality or are under an appropriate
statutory obligation of confidentiality.

## Security Measures

Processor shall implement appropriate technical and organizational
measures to ensure a level of security appropriate to the risk, including
encryption, access controls, and regular testing of such measures.
