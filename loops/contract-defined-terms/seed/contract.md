# Master Services Agreement

This Master Services Agreement (the "**Agreement**") is entered into as of
the **Effective Date** by and between the parties identified in the
signature block below.

## Definitions

- **"Agreement"** means this Master Services Agreement, together with all
  exhibits, schedules, and amendments hereto.
- **"Services"** means the professional services described in one or more
  Statements of Work executed under this Agreement.
- **"Confidential Information"** means any non-public information disclosed
  by either party that is designated as confidential or that reasonably
  should be understood to be confidential.

## Scope of Services

Provider shall perform the **Services** described in each Statement of
Work in accordance with the terms of this **Agreement**. All work shall
be completed on or before the delivery dates specified therein.

## Confidentiality

Each party shall protect the other party's **Confidential Information**
using at least the same degree of care it uses to protect its own
confidential information, but in no event less than a reasonable degree
of care.

## Term

This Agreement commences on the **Effective Date** and continues until
terminated in accordance with its terms.
